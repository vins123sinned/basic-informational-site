About Me

Hello! If you saw any of my other projects where I introduced myself, then hello, again! The name's Vinson, and throughout every single project my passion still stands with programming. The internet was and is a big part of my life, from when I first got an iPad when I was a kid to now starting Node.js again after a really long hiatus.

I can't really explain it right now, but something just feels right programming and I've never once hated doing this stuff. While there were definitely times that I've procrastinated it was usually just a bad trait of mine and nothing to do with not wanting to code or program. There is a lot that I would love to talk about with programming and myself, but I want to finish this project relatively quick so I can move on to more interesting and greater projects. All in all, I would like to express my gratitude for the internet, programming, online gaming, and everything that happened in my life to lead me here. The future may seem uncertain, But I know that if I follow my passion and desire I will get wherever I need to be, and I hope that you too can experience peace, joy, harmony, love, and all the goods things in your life. It's a bit late as I'm writing this so I'll keep it short: I sincerely hope with all of my heart you are doing great right now, whoever is reading this (even programs!), and I wish you a great rest of your life and eternity onwards! Goodbye!
